# Dyatlov Pass Incident - Sources and References

## Primary Scientific Sources

### Definitive Study

1. **Gaume, J., & Puzrin, A. M. (2021).** "Mechanisms of slab avalanche release and impact in the Dyatlov Pass incident in 1959." *Communications Earth & Environment*, 2(1), 10. https://doi.org/10.1038/s43247-020-00081-8
   - **Significance:** Definitive peer-reviewed scientific explanation
   - **Key Findings:** Computer modeling demonstrates delayed slab avalanche explains all evidence
   - **Institution:** ETH Zurich, Switzerland

2. **Puzrin, A. M., & Gaume, J. (2020).** "The mechanics of the Dyatlov Pass incident: Delayed slab avalanche as a plausible explanation." *ArXiv preprint*. arXiv:2011.14927
   - **Pre-publication version** of the 2021 study
